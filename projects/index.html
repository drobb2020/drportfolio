one EXCESSION development lab

I own a second domain named oneexcessionlab.tech. I'm using this domain as my development platform. Look for more from this domain in the future. The name Excession comes from the science fiction novel by Iain M. Banks of the same name. This is one of my favorite books.

There's an entry for 'excession' in the Oxford English Dictionary, defined as "a going out or forth." The first date of occurrence is 1655. It comes from the same Latin root as "exceed", in the sense of passing a boundary.
